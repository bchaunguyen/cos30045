About us.

We aim to develop a website that presents the reality of household energy consumption and raises public awareness about energy efficiency.

By visualising official energy consumption data, this website helps users better understand how different appliances, brands, and technologies impact electricity usage.

The goal is to support informed decision-making, encourage energy-efficient choices, and contribute to reducing energy costs and environmental impact.
